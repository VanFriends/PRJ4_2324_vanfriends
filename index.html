Who are we: 

Van Friends is an online community dedicated to van campers, nomads, and adventurers alike, providing a platform to connect, share experiences, and discover the joy of life on the road. As avid travelers ourselves, we understand the allure of the open road and the freedom that comes with exploring the world from the comfort of your own van.

 At Van Friends, we aim to foster a vibrant community of like-minded individuals who share a passion for van life. Whether you're a seasoned van dweller or just starting your journey, our platform offers a wealth of resources to enhance your travel experience.

 

Values

Teamwork. Involvement. Friends of nature. 

Vision

To create a safe space for everyone who is in the community and also for people who are interested in joining the community.

Mission

To create a platform where everyone can share their ideas and knowledge to make their van journey as pleasant as possible for each other.
