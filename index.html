How to use VanFriends:

Travelling has never been easier!

Simply choose a route from our chosen destination, proved to be the best for any van lover, and dive yourself into an amazing adventure. With VanFriends, you would stop worrying about finding the right amenities - we got them for you! On our vanmap you can find the best toilets, showers, parking spots, repair shops and many more. You can see the costs, reviews or both. If you are looking for buddy, you can find any signed VanFriend who is travelling on the same route as you. Are you ready?
