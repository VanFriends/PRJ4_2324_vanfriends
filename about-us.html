About us

 

Our company:

Van Friends is an online community dedicated to van campers, nomads, and adventurers alike, providing a platform to connect, share experiences, and discover the joy of life on the road. As avid travelers ourselves, we understand the allure of the open road and the freedom that comes with exploring the world from the comfort of your own van. 

 At Van Friends, we aim to foster a vibrant community of like-minded individuals who share a passion for van life. Whether you're a seasoned van dweller or just starting your journey, our platform offers a wealth of resources to enhance your travel experience.

Vision

In a world where campervan travellers struggle to have the right knowledge, VanFriends saves the day. The platform that not only acknowledges problems but thinks in solutions. Where information is right there, in your pocket. Where you are in charge to chase your routes. While guiding you from being travellers to becoming adventurers, where no unnecessary stops will be made, and gas will be saved.

Mission

We aim to make VanFriends into the platform that holds all the information a van traveller needs. We achieve this by sharing routes throughout Europe with all the information such as, where is the safest place to stop and sleep, where to get food, where to find clean sanitary and a nice warm shower, but also new roads to drive, new places to go and new sights to see. With this as our starting point, our mission is become a library of information specifically for our community of campervan travellers. With all their information organized in the same place, to spare time and effort searching in multiple places. This will maximize the campervan travel experience, to enjoy speding time, doing more important things. .

Friends of Nature

As campervan travellers we cherish spending time surrounded by fresh air and nature. The feeling of freedom and being able to go anywhere at any time, while always having everything you need at hand. The only thing you require is provided by earth itself. The use of eco-friendly products is VanFriends way. We want our community to feel as close to nature as we do. To enjoy and appreciate the plants, flowers and trees as well as the blue skies and even the rain. To think outside of the box and see the beauty in the things surrounding us.

Nomadic hub

We value working together, as teamwork makes the dreams work. VanFriends succeeds in this by providing knowledge and experiences to help everyone create their most memorable moments. It is also Important to highlight less picturesque events to prepare for uncomfortable situations. The experience of traveling is more than going from point A to B. It is an adventure that can change a person's life and set new horizons. Our community breaks the travelling norms by bringing people together, no matter the circumstances. After all, together we accomplish more than alone.

Involvement

Understanding the challenges our VanFriends face is one of our main priorities. Surrounding them with a trusted and safe place where they feel understood. We want to hear their stories, struggles, opinions, what they dislike, and what they love. We solve these issues in a hands-on manner with creative solutions to better the van experiences and make it even more pleasant and fun. We care for the missing piece of the puzzle. So, forget about your struggles, VanFriends got you.

Evolving

The world around us changes, so does the needs of all travellers. It is important for us to keep evolving and embrace the change around us, that’s why we commit to provide the latest travel innovation for our VanFriends. We always keep working to improve and add new features, as we believe that we can advance, learn and invent new things to improve for and with our community. We drive forward to better the road and keep evolving!
